Riccardo Porrini

I am a Software Developer at 7Pixel and also a PhD Student at DISCo (Dipartimento di Informatica, Sistemistica e Comunicazione), University of Milano-Bicocca. My research interests include Entity Reconciliation, Dataspaces, Semantic Web and Linked Data. As Software Developer I usually develop eCommerce enterprise software, more specifically in online product prices comparison. I have been working on the back-end software for TrovaPrezzi, the major italian Online Price Comparison Engine.

Projects

Result Oriented Autocomplete

An Autocompletion System based on a light-weight semi-structured data autocompletion matching algorithm, designed specifically for the eCommerce domain. The system has been developed during my MSc thesis. Currently the system is online and you can try it by typing an autocompletion query in the text box below (results are in Italian language). We also evaluated our approach to autocompletion in terms of effectiveness, and also defined a dataset based on graded relevance judgement.
